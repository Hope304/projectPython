MISSION: Training of competent future leaders who are worthy in character and sound judgment for the agro-industrial sector and for the application of all aspects of engineering towards providing solutions to global food insecurity and economic problems. The Department of Food and Agricultural Engineering is one of the foundation departments established when the College of Engineering and Technology, was set up in 2009. At inception, the department was to run two programs at the undergraduate level. These were Food Engineering and Agricultural Engineering. The Department is striving to be among the most popular departments in the Universitythrough the development of curriculum and training methods that are responsive to the needs of society Department of Food & Agricultural Engineering HEAD OF DEPARTMENT WELCOME ADDRESS Training competent future leaders for the agro-industrial sector to provide solutions to global food security and economic problems Rasheed Amao BUSARI (Ph.D) Goals/Objectives To produce graduates with sufficient knowledge to apply engineering principles to the practice of agriculture including mechanization, crop preservation and storage, irrigation and drainage, farm structures, and rural electrification. To train engineers with sufficiently broad-based knowledge to tackle day-to-day agricultural engineering problems and continuously relate their acquired skills to the needs of society To produce graduates through a well-established training facility and curriculum that reflect professionalism in Agricultural Engineering Practice. To produce graduates that are versatile in the operation, maintenance, and repair of labour-saving equipment that are used in agriculture and systems/processes that minimize environmental degradation.
