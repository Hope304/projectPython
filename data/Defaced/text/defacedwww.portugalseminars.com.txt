His career has been mostly in publishing, as founder and CEO of the Open Media Group; publishers of the Portugal Resident newspaper and Essential Algarve magazine amongst other titles.Whilst continuing in his role at Open Media, Bruce also has interests in real estate. Alongside his business activities, he is also a member of the board of directors of the Lisbon based British Portuguese Chamber of Commerce. Bruce is the host and a speaker at the Living in Portugal and Living in the Algarve events. Jorge PereiraSales & Marketing Director - Fine & Country Jorge was born in Angola but grew up in Setúbal near Lisbon before moving to Geneva, Switzerland where he completed a degree in Tourism Management. He lived and worked for five years in Ukraine before moving to Portugal in 2012 and started his career in real estate in 2015. Jorge has been with Fine & Country since 2018, initially as general manager of the central Algarve office and more recently as sales & marketing director for the Algarve region. Whilst based in the Algarve, Jorge works closely with his colleagues at the Fine & Country offices elsewhere in Portugal, from Porto in the north to Lisbon, Estoril and Comporta. Cecile GonçalvesDirector - Libertas Group Cecile is a passionate real estate expert with a degree in business management who has worked in property for over 25 years. Here career started in her native France and she has also worked in Spain and Brazil. She moved to Portugal some 5 years to join the board of Libertas Real Estate Group, a Portuguese company founded by her father Antonio Gonçalves that has been responsible for several hundred thousand square feet of development in Portugal over the last 30 years. Libertas focuses on sustainable development, displayed through the design and construction process which is environmentally certified. With a diversified approach, the company has already built and developed more than 4,000 properties in Portugal. Projects are located in the Algarve (Faro, Albufeira and Ferragudo) in the Lisbon Area (city center, Alcochete and Seixal) and on the west coast in Figueira da Foz. João Richard CostaChief Commercial Officer - Ombria Resort João has been managing the Ombria Resort sales team in Loulé since 2016. João has extensive management experience in real estate, having worked in several mixed-use luxury resort developments, namely at Pine Cliffs Resort, Praia D’El Rey Golf & Beach Resort and Westin CampoReal Golf Resort & Spa, in Portugal.Ombria Resort is a luxury low-density sustainable development surrounded by nature, in the heart of the authentic Algarve and less than 25 minutes from the coast and Faro airport. It includes a 5-star hotel, an 18-hole golf course and several different types of properties for sale. Currently under construction, the completion of its first phase is scheduled for spring 2023. Jaan-Claire Rodrigues Senior Property Consultant – Quinta do Lago Real Estate Jaan-Claire Rodrigues is Senior Property Consultant at Quinta do Lago Real Estate. Born in England, she travelled to Portugal at the young age of 5. Fluent in Portuguese, English and French, Jaan-Claire joined Quinta do Lago Real Estate in 2014 and puts her heart and soul into the job. Married and with two daughters, she enjoys travelling and taking re-energising power walks along the beautiful nature reserve of Quinta do Lago. Quinta do Lago Real Estate is the Official Quinta do Lago Real Estate Agency, winner of the Best Real Estate Agency Single Office in Portugal in the European Property Awards. Quinta do Lago Real Estate are proud to offer expertise and guidance on property purchase and sales within the resort. The local and natural experts of Quinta do Lago, the team is located at Roundabout 2, offering unparalleled professional service in Europe’s most coveted destination. Brian BernalDirector of Business Development - Currencies Direct Brian helps set the standards in the foreign exchange industry by delivering the best and most comprehensive services to clients. His passion for financial markets and helping clients achieve their objectives has helped Brian establish close relationships with clients and partners. His knowledge of foreign exchange, financial markets, global banking, investments, securities, and global marketplaces is extensive while his hands-on experiences super exceed the textbook written norms. Founded in 1996, Currencies Direct has grown to become the leader in the FX industry with regards to transfers for property purchases and immigration. With over 20 global offices across 5 continents, including Portugal and the US, Currencies Direct is well equipped to assist with your foreign exchange and international transfer needs. Dr. Ricardo FerreiraExecutive Director - Martinez Echevarria & Ferreira law firmDr. Ricardo Ferreira is the country director for Portugal of Martinez Eschevarria, the largest law firm in Andalucia, Spain as well as in the Algarve where it has offices in Quinta do Lago, Vilamoura, Albufeira and Lagos. The company also has Portuguese offices in Lisbon, Estoril and Porto. In Spain, there are offices in Madrid as well as in ten locations across Andalucia. Outside of the Iberian Peninsula the firm also has a presence in Turkey with offices in Istanbul and Bodrum. Internationally, Martinez Eschevarria employs more than 250 professionals. Dr. Ricardo Ferreira gained his law degree from the University of Lisbon in 1999, going on to earn postgraduate degrees in both commercial law, tax planning and accounting and finance law, from the University of Lisbon and the Catholic University of Law respectively. Since 2012 he has been mainly dedicated to the coordination and management of the Martinez Echevarria & Ferreira law firm’s offices in Portugal focusing on Real Estate Law and Tax law. Alda FilipeReal Estate and Marketing Director - Kronos Homes Alda Filipe is the Real Estate and Marketing Director for Kronos Homes Portugal & Managing Director for Kronos’ Resort Development portfolio in the Algarve. With over 20 years of hands-on experience in the sales and marketing of leading luxury golf & beach resorts and real estate developments, Alda’s market knowledge is unparalleled. She has enabled thousands of clients from all over the world to invest in and/or move to Portugal. Her high standards and proven track record combined with a client-focused approach enables buyers to fulfil their ambitions. Kronos Real Estate is a fully integrated investment and development company with offices in Spain, Portugal, Luxembourg and the UK. It is today one of the most prestigious Iberian residential developers with over 15.000 units built. Working with some of the best architects in Iberia, design, sustainability, quality and innovation are the pillars of Kronos Homes’ puredesign philosophy, a commitment to elevate residential architecture to its maximum exponential. Henrique PinheiroFounder & CEO - Medal Insurance brokers Henrique founded Medal Insurance in 1996 and has built the business with his family and a dedicated team to be Portugal’s leading insurance brokerage for expats. His presentation at our Living in the Portugal seminars will focus on health insurance and healthcare in Portugal for foreign residents. Medal was founded in the Algarve where it has three offices covering the whole coast. Medal also has offices in Cascais covering the Greater Lisbon area. Alex OhnonaPartner - Vida CapitalAlex Ohnona is a Partner at Vida Advisors, responsible for overseeing strategic initiatives related to asset selection, concept development, design, and hospitality. He previously served as Managing Partner at Solis Capital, aiding real estate operators with expansion plans and capital raising. Alex was the VP at Selina, the fastest international hospitality expansion to date, where he spearheaded product development, managed global partnerships, and contributed to significant portfolio expansion. Alex played a pivotal role within the Chairman of Strategy Office at HCL He holds a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from Babson College. Hakan KodalChairman - Ando Living / OptylonKreaHakan Kodal, Chairman of Ando Living and OptylonKrea, is renowned for his 30-year tenure in investment management and real estate. As the Founding Chairman of various real estate organizations and companies including his fund management partnership with Merrill Lynch, he led 1.8 billion real estate investments in Europe. Hakan has evolved into a recognized authority in Portugal, with a focus on invesment management and branded residences”. Sara AllenReal Estate Director - Elegant Group Sara Allen is the Real Estate Director of the Elegant Group who own Martinhal Family Hotels & Resorts. The Founders of the group developed the Martinhal Resort in Sagres in the Algarve from scratch and have successfully expanded the “Martinhal” brand across 3 other properties in the best and most prestigious locations in Portugal – with one more branded hotel residence coming up in Lisbon. They are also working on commercial real-estate projects and an Education Hub project in Lisbon, between the Park of Nations and the airport. The school, United Lisbon International School successfully opened in September 2020. The school forms part of a wider Edu Hub. Soon, the Edu Hub will be filled with other innovators, tech companies and an academy that all have something to contribute to the ecosystem of education. The school is a K-12 International School with an American-based Curriculum and IB diploma. Luis DuarteChief Sales Officer - Global International Relocations Luís joined Global International Relocations in 2008, just 8 months after the company started trading. At the time he had just returned from the US and was settling in Portugal, a move that he considers to be the best one of his life. 14 years later he reflects on an exciting journey and continues to learn more and more about relocating international clients to Portugal from all around the world. Today, GIR employs over 100 people with three offices and is the number 1 mobility company in Portugal. The company offers high-quality services in the areas of international moving, relocation, immigration, property services, special transport of pets and artworks, excess baggage service, storage and management of business archives. Duarte Calheiro e MenezesPartner & Fund Manager - BlueCrow Duarte acquired extensive experience over five years trading futures and options, before joining BlueCrow in 2014. This challenge was propelled by a common goal of maximizing investments in commercial companies, asset management portfolios and business projects with significant potential for growth. With a background in economics (Degree and Master's from Católica Lisbon University), he is specialized in financial asset management and risk hedging, in portfolio management, and in asset structuring. BlueCrow Capital was founded in 2010, currently operating with two separate financial licenses regulated by the Portuguese Securities Market Commission (CMVM). The emphasis remains on the creation of investment and divestment strategies, financing projects at different stages of their respective maturity, and the monitoring and management of financial assets. Duarte’s presentation at the Living in Portugal events in California will focus on Golden Visa-eligible investment funds as an alternative to the direct purchase of property for the purpose of citizenship by investment.
