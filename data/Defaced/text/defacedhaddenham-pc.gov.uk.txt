News Events Contact PreviousNext12345Report a highways fault Welcome to the website of Haddenham & Aldreth Parish Council Welcome to the website of Haddenham Parish Council, Cambridgeshire. Our Parish consists of the villages of Haddenham & Aldreth as well as Hillrow Causeway. Haddenham Parish Council has 13 Councillors who serve a four year term. The Council meets monthly (except for August) and holds it’s Annual Meeting in May. The schedule of meetings for the coming year are shown on the Agendas & Minutes page. Agendas are posted on the village noticeboards and on this website at least three days before each meeting is convened. Members of the public are most welcome, and indeed encouraged, to attend and provision is made at all meetings for public participation.
