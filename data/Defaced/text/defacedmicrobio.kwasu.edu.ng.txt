The department is headed by a professor who gives academic leadership, and guidance and is current in the direction of teaching, learning and research in Microbiology. He is a trained industrial microbiologist and is supported by other academic staff in many areas of Microbiology such as Microbial Genetics, Environmental Microbiology, Microbial Physiology, Soil Microbiology, Mycology, Food Microbiology, Bacteriology, Virology, Industrial Microbiology, and Analytical Microbiology. Philosophy,Mission,Vision,Objectives, Goals Statement Philosophy: To equip students with knowledge, skills and techniques in the various areas of specialization in Microbiology which are relevant to the needs and challenges of the country. Mission: To be the fulcrum of a knowledge-driven society, building human competencies and determination through the instrumentality of deep knowledge of Microbiological ideas. Vision: To be foremost in expanding the frontiers of Microbiological knowledge, and relevant in every sector that encompasses practical experience in Microbiology. Objectives: • To produce world-class microbiologists that would provide some of the most accessible tools for probing the nature of life sciences. • To deal with many important theoretical and practical problems in science, medicine and analytical work. • To play major roles in the study of soil fertility, animal production molecular biology and large-scale industrial processes. • To enable students to develop invaluable generic skills that will improve their employability and enterprise as well as personal and professional development. Goals: • To produce high-level manpower in the sciences through the acquisition of requisite skills and knowledge for national development. • To develop in science graduates a sense of inquiry, capacity for independent research and motivation to extend the frontiers of science and technology. • To produce graduates who will be adequately equipped for relevance in the global knowledge economy. • To produce graduates who can apply appropriate scientific principles for solving problems for the promotion of human well-being. • To produce manpower with optimal competencies and skills to function effectively in the academia and the private sector. Our Programmes HEAD OF DEPARTMENT WELCOME ADDRESS Microbiology provides explanations for the activities of unseen creatures (microbes) for life processes up to the molecular level. Passing through the course successfully requires utmost dedication and diligence. Programmes/Activities The department runs the following programmes: i. B.Sc. Microbiology., ii. M.Sc. Microbiology., iii. PGD Microbiology and iv. Ph.D. degrees in Microbiology. Achievement and Milestone KWASU is the first University in Nigeria to be approved to have a Student Chapter of American Society for Microbiology.
