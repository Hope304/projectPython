We strive to deliver excellence in early learning to all of the children in our care, providing an environment where parents and teachers work together, assisting each child’s development to reach their full potential. We have a team of qualified and experienced staff members and operate in two locations. Both our centres comprise of three separate rooms and cater for children aged from 6 weeks to 12 years. In addition to this, we also offer before and after school care. We offer a comprehensive school readiness program in conjunction with holding many annual celebrations throughout the year to entertain the children.
