EAP adheres to the principles the Creative Commons and favours Open Access publishing. EAP aims at providing world-class services for its authors, editors, conference organizers and readers by offering the up-to-date services they may expect from a professional publisher. EAP publishes high quality, peer-reviewed conference articles, journals and books, primarily in electronic format on the Internet but also in print and other media.
