The Agricultural Materials Traders & Producers Association of Jordan was first established in 1982 with the clear objective of introducing and promoting new technologies in the agricultural sector aiming at the improvements of its contribution to the national gross domestic product. A prime objective of the association, as well, is to promote the appropriate and responsible balance between members benefits and good relation with authorities as well as professional associations. Members: · Importers and local manufacturers of horticultural inputs. · Exporters of horticultural products. · Retailers of horticultural inputs · Nurseries.
