It has 12 wards & 2.49 Sq Km in area. The population is 20999 as per census 2011 (19310 as per census 2001). This municipality has been come to its existence in the year of 1983 (10th Feb,1983). Before this the town was controlled and administered by the Town Committee. First general election was held in 28th May 1995. Tufanganj Municipal town is under the administrative Jurisdiction of Cooch Behar District. The Town Tufanganj is situated at a distance of 24 Kms. East of the District town. It is surrounded by the state of Assam in the East, Doors in the Northern side, Bangladesh southern part, Siliguri and Jalpaiguri in the western side of Tufanganj Town. In the eastern side one major river Raidak is passing through the Tufanganj Town. Most of the people about 70% are fully depend on agriculture that is the primary sector.
