The critical mass of the embryo of the Business School was the KWASU Master of Business Administration (MBA) program. KWASU Business School was established to provide specialized education and training in business and management-related disciplines. The School is designed to equip students with the knowledge and skills needed to excel in the world of business, entrepreneurship, and leadership. We are committed to fostering leadership, innovation, and ethical business practices through rigorous academic programs, industry engagement, and community outreach. Vision:Our vision is to be recognized as a preeminent business school that drives positive societal and economic impact through transformative education, cutting-edge research, and a diverse and inclusive community of students, faculty, and staff. Goals:Our goals and objectives are as follows: Academic Excellence: Strive for academic excellence by maintaining rigorous curriculum standards, promoting faculty research, and continuously improving teaching methodologies. Student Success: Ensure the success of our students by providing a supportive learning environment, mentoring opportunities, and career development resources. Research and Innovation: Foster a culture of research and innovation among faculty and students, with a focus on addressing real-world business challenges. Global Engagement: Expand international collaborations and partnerships to provide students with global perspectives and opportunities for international experiences. Diversity and Inclusion: Promote diversity and inclusion within the School's community, ensuring a welcoming and respectful environment for all. Community Outreach: Engage with the local and global business communities to bridge the gap between theory and practice, and contribute to economic development. Ethical Leadership: Instill ethical values and responsible leadership in our students, emphasizing the importance of corporate social responsibility. Continuous Improvement: Regularly assess and improve our programs, faculty development, and infrastructure to meet evolving business education needs. Alumni Engagement: Maintain strong connections with alumni, leveraging their expertise and networks to benefit current students and the School's reputation. Sustainability: Promote sustainability practices within the School and encourage the integration of sustainable business principles into the curriculum.
