TV Aerial Cable A TV aerial cable, also known as a coaxial cable, is an essential component for receiving television signals. It is designed to transfer high-frequency signals from an antenna or cable outlet to a TV set. The cable consists of a copper conductor surrounded by insulation and a metallic shield, which protects the signal from interference and ensures optimal transmission quality. TV aerial cable come in various lengths and connector types, such as F-type or BNC connectors, allowing compatibility with different devices. These cables enable viewers to enjoy clear, reliable TV reception by establishing a reliable link between the antenna and the television.
