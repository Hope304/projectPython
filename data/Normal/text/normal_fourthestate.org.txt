Vision A world in which people are equipped with the news and information they need to make wise decisions about their lives, their communities, their societies, and their governments. Mission To contribute to a healthy society by fostering, supporting and incubating a sustainable and vibrant free press. Core Values Truth, Transparency and Community define and represent the principles, standards and practices that the Fourth Estate aspires to in our stewardship of journalism and the public trust. Beliefs We believe that an independent, sustainable free press is built on established and equitable free-market principles.
