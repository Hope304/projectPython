The New East Digital Archive was created to preserve the legacy of The Calvert Journal: an online publication covering culture and innovation across Eastern Europe, the Balkans, the Caucasus, and Central Asia between 2013 and 2022. The New East Digital Archive aims to celebrate the unique cultural era covered by The Calvert Journal and ensure that its work remains accessible to researchers, academics, and the public.
