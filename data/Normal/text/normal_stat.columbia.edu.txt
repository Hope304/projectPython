Stat GR5399: Statistical Fieldwork Learn more PhD Program The PhD program prepares students for research careers in probability and statistics in both academia and industry. The first year of the program is devoted to training in theoretical statistics, applied statistics, and probability. In the following years, students take advanced topics courses and seminars.
