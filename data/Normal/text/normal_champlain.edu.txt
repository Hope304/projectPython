View All Majors Accounting As the link that connects business, accounting professionals are in high demand in a broad range of industries. An Accounting degree from Champlain positions you for versatility and career growth. Bachelors of Science - BS Accounting: Accelerated Earn your bachelor’s degree in Accounting in just three years; Champlain’s renowned Accounting major can be completed ahead of schedule. Bachelors of Science - BS Animation New frontiers in visual effects, virtual realities, and animated storytelling are waiting to be discovered. With Champlain's BFA in Animation, you'll start exploring on day one. Bachelors of Fine Arts - BFA Applied Mathematics Focus on the math you love while learning how to make use of it in almost any field. You’ll explore careers in math and graduate with specific professional skills. Bachelors of Science - BS Applied Sustainability Saving the world is a full-time job. Learn how to create and maintain sustainable systems for communities and environments. Bachelors of Science - BS Broadcast Media Production Build a rich background in producing and directing studio- and field-based nonfiction content through a wide array of hands-on video, audio, and writing courses. Bachelors of Science - BS Business Administration Take four years of immersive and practical business education that’s tailored to suit your values and career goals. Bachelors of Science - BS Computer & Digital Forensics Our award-winning Computer & Digital Forensics major is the premier program in the country for those who want to go into the exciting digital forensics field. Bachelors of Science - BS Computer Networking & Cybersecurity Learn firsthand how cyberattacks are staged against networks. Our hands-on lab and virtual infrastructure give you experience managing situations involving sensitive data. Bachelors of Science - BS Computer Science & Innovation In addition to core computer science, this major covers the latest technological developments, teaching adaptability in the face of ever-changing languages and technologies. Bachelors of Science - BS Creative Media This major distinguishes itself from other fine art programs in the country with its cross-disciplinary focus and emphasis on the business aspect of artistic endeavors. Bachelors of Fine Arts - BFA Criminology & Criminal Justice With a focus on social justice and the flexibility to follow your passions, Champlain's Criminology & Criminal Justice program will immerse you in the field's full range of possibilities. Bachelors of Science - BS Data Analytics A distinctive part of this major is our Data Analytics Hub, where you'll work with mentors on real-world data sets, allowing you to hone the most in-demand data analytics skills. Bachelors of Science - BS Degree Design Lab Design your own major from subject areas across the College. This program will foster innovation, autonomy, and applied creativity as well an industry-specific skill set. Bachelors of Science - BS Digital Humanities Designed with the future in mind, Champlain's BS in Digital Humanities can strategically prepare you to pioneer new and uncharted professions in our digital world. Bachelors of Science - BS Education Become an exemplary teacher with a deep set of skills. You’ll observe local classrooms in your first year, working your way up to full-time student teaching. Bachelors of Science - BS Filmmaking This program is tailored to help you develop your creative and technical filmmaking skills while also teaching fluency in the vital business aspects of film production. Bachelors of Fine Arts - BFA Finance A work-based approach allows you to develop strong financial expertise and innovative management ability. Senior year, you’ll manage a portion of the College’s endowment. Bachelors of Science - BS Game Art Explore traditional media and visual art, learn 2-D and 3-D graphics and animation, and use all those skills to work with peers to create full games. Bachelors of Fine Arts - BFA Game Business & Publishing Learn the ins and outs of the game industry by immersing yourself in it for four years. Bachelors of Science - BS Game Design Learn to map out, develop, and create the gameplay experience as a systems, level, or narrative designer as you collaborate with peers in a professional studio environment. Bachelors of Science - BS Game Production Management This major is a hands-on deep dive into the business side of the game industry. You’ll learn to organize, motivate, and oversee creative teams for game development. Bachelors of Science - BS Game Programming Gain a versatile, in-demand skill set: apply the challenge of software creation to the context of game development while working within a professional game studio setting. Bachelors of Science - BS Game Sound Design Learn music composition, scoring, voiceover recording, game engine scripting, and more, all while working directly with game engines on a game development team. Bachelors of Science - BS Graphic Design & Visual Communication In this forward-thinking BFA program, you’ll become an innovative, creative problem solver—mixing words and images to communicate ideas visually across variable media. Bachelors of Fine Arts - BFA Interaction Design Exploring user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, you’ll learn how to create successful interactions between humans and devices. Bachelors of Science - BS Interactive Narrative for Games & Digital Media The human brain is wired to participate. Learn to write and design narrative in ways that bring your audience or users into the story. Bachelors of Science - BS Law As a student, you’ll be doing the types of work legal professionals do every day, giving you a tremendous advantage in your career and law school applications. Bachelors of Science - BS Marketing Begin earning industry-standard certifications like Google Analytics before your sophomore year. This cutting-edge, hands-on major will put you ahead of the marketing curve. Bachelors of Science - BS Marketing Communication Build expertise in the professional space where communication and marketing overlap. Bachelors of Science - BS Psychology As you study psychology, you’ll build real experience in professional work and research; you’ll be immersed in ways to apply your degree across various industries. Bachelors of Science - BS Social Work With our combination of broad social work learning and extensive field experiences, you'll be prepared for a career building a better world for the people who need it most. Bachelors of Social Work - BSW Undeclared Business As an undeclared student in Business, you'll have a chance to explore areas of business you never knew existed while you learn fundamental business practices. Undeclared Major - UNDC Undeclared Communication & Creative Media As an undeclared student in Communication & Creative Media (CCM), you will be able to explore your interests in different fields before you choose a creative career path. Undeclared Major - UNDC Undeclared General (No Subject Area) You may apply as a general undeclared student or apply undeclared in Social Innovation, Information Technology, Communication & Creative Media, or Business. Undeclared Major - UNDC Undeclared Information Technology & Sciences As an undeclared student in Information Technology & Sciences (ITS), you’ll take relevant classes starting in year one to quickly discover your true passion and potential. Undeclared Major - UNDC Undeclared School of Social Innovation As an undeclared student in the School of Social Innovation, you'll be supported in finding the right major for you, so you can start making a difference. Undeclared Major - UNDC Writing & Publishing (BS) Get paid to do what you love. You’ll develop your writing and editing skills and become comfortable with the professional aspects of writing.
