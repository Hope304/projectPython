The Drury University MissionDrury is an independent university, church-related, grounded in the liberal arts tradition, and committed to personalized education in a community of scholars who value the arts of teaching and learning. Education at Drury seeks: To cultivate spiritual sensibilities and imaginative faculties as well as ethical insight and critical thought; To foster the integration of theoretical and practical knowledge; and To liberate persons to participate responsibly in and contribute to life in a global community.
