To get started, create a free geocaching account and download the official Geocaching® app or use a GPS device. 3 steps to begin your adventure 1. Create an account Create an account online or through the Geocaching® app to view a map of geocaches near you. 2. Find a geocache Use the app to navigate to a geocache nearby. Don’t forget to bring a pen! 3. Share your experience Once you find the geocache, sign and date the logbook. Place the geocache back where you found it and log your experience online.
