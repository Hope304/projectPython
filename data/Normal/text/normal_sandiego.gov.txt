A Message From the Mayor San Diego is a special place with limitless potential, where everyone deserves equal access to opportunity, happiness, and a bright future. Our City is here to help foster those ideals in every neighborhood. It exists to serve you and be a resource for you, your family, and your community. Working together, we can create a San Diego that is responsive, transparent, and inclusive for all of us.
