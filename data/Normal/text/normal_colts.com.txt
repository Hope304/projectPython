looking forward to 2024 Advertising Radio & Podcasts Free Agent Preview: Taven Bryan and Tyquan Lewis Matt Taylor, JJ Stankevitz and Casey Vallier break down the free agency looks of defensive lineman Taven Bryan and Tyquan Lewis. The fellas discuss what Bryan brought to the Colts when Grover Stewart was suspended for six games and marvel at Lewis' efficiency at disrupting the running game and opposing quarterbacks in limited defensive snaps in 2023. 2024 NFL Scouting Combine: AFC South Roundtable We huddle in the division with the voices from the AFC South. Matt Taylor and Casey Vallier talk with Marc Vandermeer, the voice of the Texans, breaking down how CJ Stroud wowed in Houston in his rookie season. Matt and Jeffrey Gorman welcome the voice of the Jaguars, Frank Frangie, to discuss how the Jaguars are poised to bounce back after losing five of their last six games to close out last season. Lastly, Matt and Jeffrey speak with Mike Keith, the voice of the Titans, regarding Will Levis taking over at quarterback in 2024 and new head coach Brian Callahan taking over in Nashville.
