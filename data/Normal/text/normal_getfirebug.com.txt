Firebug Thank you, Firebug. You made the web amazing for all! The story of Firefox and Firebug are synonymous with the rise of the web. Today, the work pioneered by the Firebug community through the last 12 years lives on in Firefox Developer Tools. Switch to the latest version of Firefox and see the very latest devtools updates.
