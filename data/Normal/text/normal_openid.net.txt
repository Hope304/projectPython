What is OpenID DISCOVER What is OpenID HOW OpenID Works HOW OpenID Works JOIN The OpenID Foundation JOIN The OpenID Foundation Our mission is to lead the global community in creating identity standards that are secure, interoperable and privacy-preserving. Founded in 2007, the OpenID Foundation (OIDF) is a global open standards body committed to helping people assert their identity wherever they choose. We are global vibrant community where identity peers and thought leaders convene to craft the identity ecosystems of tomorrow. Enable real-world identity use cases Find an existing working group, explore all the specifications you can develop with, and certify your implementation using our processes and tools in your journey to adopting open and interoperable identity standards.
