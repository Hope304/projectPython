PTSD is a mental health problem that some people develop after experiencing or witnessing a life-threatening or traumatic event. If symptoms last more than a few months, it may be PTSD. The good news is that there are effective treatments. Understand PTSD Learn about symptoms, types of trauma, common reactions, and related problems.
