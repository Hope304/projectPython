With 57 States from Europe, Central Asia and North America, the OSCE is the world's largest regional security organization. Learn more What We Do Our activities cover a wide range of security issues such as conflict prevention to fostering economic development, ensuring the sustainable use of natural resources & promoting the full respect of human rights and fundamental freedoms.
