The New York Times Web Archive The New York Times Web Archive Preserving the original web presentation of articles, interactive projects, and other pages from years of internet publishing. How does this work? This site hosts a copy of the HTML of NYTimes.com pages from when they were first published, preserving their design and original presentation for posterity. For more information, to report an error, or if you are interested in collaborating with or using the archive, please contact us at archive@nytimes.com.
