ARD publishes basic, clinical, and translational scientific research, including the most important recommendations for the management of various conditions. Free lay summaries are provided for selected research articles and all EULAR recommendations. You can keep up with the latest news via X and Facebook. Listen to the ARD Podcast and subscribe in all podcast platforms, including Apple Podcasts, Amazon Music and Spotify.
