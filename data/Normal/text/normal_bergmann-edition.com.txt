Read More 1 / of 2 View all ABOUT US Bergmann Edition, based in Aarhus, Denmark, is Europe’s leading publisher of new and rare music for classical guitar. Founded in 2015 by Allan Bergmann Jensen, the Bergmann imprint now represents over 160 contemporary composers working in all genres from experimental avant-garde to traditionally melodic and tonal. We publish some of the guitar world’s greatest living composers, arrangers and players, in high quality beautifully engraved editions. We also publish a selective range of teaching literature.Our catalogue includes hundreds of works for solo classical guitar: the majority of them are new compositions, but we also publish new arrangements of historic music, from medieval to twentieth-century. In addition, we bring you pieces composed or arranged for various sizes of guitar ensemble, for rare combinations of classical guitar with other instruments, and for guitar with voice, chamber orchestra or full orchestra.
