Coaching Youth Middle Distance Runners is a book for coaches who train athletes aged between 6 and 18 years old to participate in running events between 800 and 5,000 m. Topics discussed include nutritional concerns, psychological considerations, effective training methods, and competition strategies. Special attention is given to potential differences between genders. An appendix includes selections for further reading. Image credit More featured children's books...
