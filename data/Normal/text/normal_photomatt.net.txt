The frame is there‚Äôs a game, each person gets a card: Be the Host, Help the Host, or Neutral. You cannot change cards during the course of your day or week. If you do not feel aligned with your card, you need to change divisions within Automattic. If you‚Äôre Be the Host, you are hyper-competitive. You are trying to make the case to a customer for why they should host with you and not consider anyone else. You want every host to be as successful as possible, because the real threat is from the Big Proprietary folks outside, who steal all your good ideas and don‚Äôt let you touch them again. You want to get to know every WordPress in the world, however it grew up, and help it out by selling it attachments. Neutral treats everyone equally, either because they don‚Äôt care (Day One, Pocket Casts, et cetera don‚Äôt have a horse in this race) or because they are a support function like finance or HR.
