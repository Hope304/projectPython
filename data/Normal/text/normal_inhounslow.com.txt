Join in, and help us make Hounslow what’s happening in London this summer.Why Hounslow?The London Borough of Hounslow is a vibrant, exciting place to visit, stay and explore, with many hidden gems. Miles of rivers – including the Thames, canals, nature reserves and parks, bustling town centres, and a fantastic leisure offer.Four animated town centres: Feltham, Hounslow, Brentford, and Chiswick provide fantastic days out, each with a unique offer to visitors. Bordering with Richmond, Ealing, and Heathrow Airport in Hillingdon, the borough boasts some of the most historic and biodiverse open spaces in London and a diverse population that ensures an incredible selection of specialist shops and cuisines from around the world. Experience incredible heritage from Highway Men, the Rothschild’s first Country House, the Battle of Brentford, and boat building on the Thames; to being the home of Hogarth, Mo Farah, and Freddie Mercury. inBrentfordinChiswickinFelthaminHounslowMake your destination: Hounslow Brentford Chiswick Feltham OutdoorHounslow offers some fantastic outdoor spaces and activities Learn More StayHounslow is the gateway to London, ready to satisfy every traveller’s needs.
