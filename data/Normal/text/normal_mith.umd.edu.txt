We also teach courses and hosts events for campus and public communities in support of our core research mission.You might know us through our work on the Unlocking the Airwaves project, on the Shelley-Godwin Archive, with AADHum (African American Digital and Experimental Humanities @ UMD), or through some of the other work of our faculty and staff members.The MITH website is currently being redeveloped in public.
