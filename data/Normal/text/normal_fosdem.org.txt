FOSDEM 2024 - Home fosdem-2024 Home About News Schedule Stands Volunteer Practical Search: beer devrooms open source 8000+ hackers lightning talks 861 lectures Brussels / 3 & 4 February 2024 schedule WELCOME TO FOSDEM 2024 FOSDEM is a free event for software developers to meet, share ideas and collaborate. Every year, thousands of developers of free and open source software from all over the world gather at the event in Brussels. You don't need to register. Just turn up and join in! Thank You! Thank You to everyone who contributed in any way to the success of FOSDEM 2024! Videos We are adding a video of each talk to its web page when it is ready. See the processing status of individual talks. For everything available so far, look in the right-hand column of the complete schedule, or download from video.fosdem.org/2024 (arranged by room name). Feedback Please use the links under each event to provide feedback to the speakers. Send more general feedback to feedback@fosdem.org. All your feedback, good or bad, and suggestions for future years will be greatly appreciated. See you in 2025!
