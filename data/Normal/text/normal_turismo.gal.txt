Android iOS The Way of Saint James in Galicia As part of the Smart Camino project, we have created a pocket travel guide to accompany you on your journey, a pioneering mobile app that seeks to become the leader in its segment. Android iOS Galicia Birding Official app with birding routes for tourists in Galicia. The Axencia Turismo de Galicia provides this app to assist you on your visit to Galicia as a bird-watching tourism destination. It will help you thoroughly explore the most important bird-watching spots in Galicia through birding routes designed especially for tourists. Android iOS Wine tourism in Galicia on your mobile phone The purpose of this tool is to publicise all the establishments included on the wine routes of Galicia :wineries, distilleries, restaurants, hotels, entertainment companies, museums, tourist offices, specialised travel agencies, wine shops etc. Android iOS MeteoGalicia Meteogalicia, the official application that offers information on weather forecasts according to municipality for the next 10 days, on sea conditions, forecasts for all beaches in Galicia, general forecasts and tides. Android iOS Galicia’s Natural Parks A guide that lists and locates the main environmental and tourist resources in the Parque Nacional de las Illas Atlánticas (National Park of the Atlantic Islands) and the other natural parks in Galicia, through more than 500 files and almost 200 photographs. Android iOS Cultural Agenda This is an agenda prepared jointly by different departments, with the aim of bringing together the calendar of cultural events, both those organised by the regional administration and those of great interest, in a single space.
