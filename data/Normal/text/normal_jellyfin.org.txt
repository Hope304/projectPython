You can use it, study it, modify it, build it, and distribute it for free, as long as your changes are licensed the same way.Community BuiltThe project relies entirely on contributions from volunteers. Want to help out? There’s lots of ways to do so, and you don’t even have to code! See our contribution guide for more details.No FeesThe Jellyfin server and official clients are free to download, now and always. There are no costs, hidden or otherwise, to use Jellyfin, either for yourself, for your friends, or for your company. All our incidental costs are paid through donations from users like you.Privacy FocusedJellyfin has no tracking, phone-home, or central servers collecting your data. We believe in keeping our software open and transparent.
