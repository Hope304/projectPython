Apply for Admission Degree Programs Leading Research Healing Hearts Associate Professor Ethan Kung works at the forefront of cardiovascular research, applying engineering principles and modeling to clinical diagnostics and treatments for high-risk conditions like heart failure and single ventricle defects. As the director of Clemson’s Cardiovascular Modeling and Experimentation Research Laboratory, he seeks to increase collaboration among mechanical and biomedical engineers, clinicians, and the medical device industry to achieve the best possible patient outcomes.
